HOW TO PLAY SNAKE ARCADE

1. The Controls

You can control the snake's direction using your keyboard. The game responds instantly to your input to allow for high-speed maneuvers.

  • UP: Arrow Up
  • DOWN: Arrow Down
  • LEFT: Arrow Left
  • RIGHT: Arrow Right
  • START: Spacebar
  • PAUSE: 'P' Key
  • RESTART: 'R' Key

2. Gameplay Rules

The logic follows the classic arcade rules with a few modern twists:

  • Feeding: Collect the Red Pellets to grow. Each pellet adds one segment to your snake's length.
  • Golden Apple: Rare Golden Apples spawn for a short time, offering 5 points and a massive score boost.
  • Magnet Power-up: Collect Blue Power Pellets to pull food toward the snake's head for 5 seconds.
  • Obstacles: Static Blocks appear in the grid center as you score, making the perimeter strategy essential.
  • The Boundaries: Do not hit the edges of the game board. The charcoal border is solid; hitting it results in an immediate Game Over.
  • Self-Collision: As you get longer, you must avoid running into your own body. Crossing your own path will end the round.

3. Scoring & Difficulty

  • Point System: Each pellet consumed awards 1 point.
  • Speed Scaling: For every pellet eaten, the game engine slightly increases the "tick rate." This means the snake moves faster the better you play.
  • High Scores: Your best performance is saved to your browser's local storage automatically.

ADVANCED STRATEGY GUIDE

To reach the 100+ point club, try the Perimeter Strategy. By moving primarily around the edges of the 28×28 grid, you keep the center open for quick maneuvering when food spawns in tight spots. Avoid "zig-zagging" which traps your head between segments.
