How to Play

The Objective: Navigate your snake to eat the Red Food pellets. Each pellet grows your snake and increases your score.

  • Arrows: Use Arrow Keys to move the snake.
  • Spacebar: Press Space to start the game.
  • 'R' Key: Press 'R' to restart after a game over.

Note: The game speed increases as your score goes up!
